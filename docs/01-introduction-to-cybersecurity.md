## What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, programs, and data from digital attacks, unauthorized access, and damage.

It involves:
- Preventing attacks
- Detecting threats
- Responding to incidents
- Recovering from breaches

---

## Why is Cybersecurity Important?

- Increasing number of cyber attacks worldwide
- Protection of personal data and privacy
- Safeguarding businesses and governments
- Growing importance in Africa (fintech, mobile money, government digital services)
- High demand for skilled professionals (great career opportunity)
